Fruit Dip

Credit:2-Ingredient Fruit Dip by J Edin

Description:

Simple delicious dip for your favorite bite sized fruit!

Ingredients:

  • 8oz cream cheese, softened
  • 7oz jar marshmallow creme

Steps:

  1. Beat cream cheese until smooth and add marshmallow creme. Blend until smooth.
  2. Serve and enjoy.
