Sugar Cookies

Credit:3 Ingredient Sugar Cookies by TheDailyGourmet

Description:

Sugar cookies are simple to make and delicious to eat. These could not be easier.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted softened butter
  • 1/2 cup vanilla sugar
  • 1 cup self-rising flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease baking pan.
  2. In a bown, mix butter until smooth, add sugar and mix till combined. Add in flour and mix until combined.
  3. Portion dough using spoon or scoop into cookies leaving 2 inches of space between them on the sheet.
  4. Bake at 350 for 14 minutes or until cooked through
  5. Allow to cool before serving
