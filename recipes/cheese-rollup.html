Cheese Rollup

Description:

The cheese rollup is super simple and super quick to make. It always makes a nice snack that never fails to satisfy.

Ingredients:

  1. 8 4-inch round tortillas
  2. 2 cups Shredded Cheese (Any kind, but preferably pepperjack)
  3. 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Line baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. Place tortilla down, brush with melted butter, and place a handful of cheese over the surface
  4. Roll tortilla and cheese up, brush with more melted butter and set on to lined baking sheet
  5. Once all tortillas are prepared, place in 350 degree oven for 10 - 15 minutes, flipping the cheese rollups every 5 minutes until cheese is melted and tortilla is golden brown.
  6. Serve with favorite dip or hot sauce. Adding chicken or beef in with the cheese is also a great addition!
